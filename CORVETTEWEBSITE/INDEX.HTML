The All New 2019 Corvette C7 ZR1

The powerhouse

This hand-assembled supercharged 6.2L V8 small block LT5 produces a grand total of 755 horsepower and a stagering 715 pound-foot of torque(687hp at the wheels). This crazy power pushes the top speed of the Corvette to 212MPH or 341KPH. The supercharger also forces 4.6 more psi then the the Z06's. It is also passed through intercoolers with double the heat-transfer capacity. All this power pushes this Corvette to a 10.8 seconds quarter mile at 135mph

Aerodynamics

Even the small wing on this car has 20% more than it's little brother, the Z06. The larger wing avalible in the ZTK package provides 950lbs of down force which is 60% more than the Z06 with Z07 package.

Aesthetics

The looks haven't changed too much from the Z06. The aerodynamics have changed the hood though.Deeper channels between the headlights and carbon fibre topped supercharger make this ZR1 distinguishable from any distance. Once the hood is open you notice they had to cut a hole in it to fit this massive supercharger on this car. Chevy also added cardon fibre all over this car. The dash is coated in it. On the exterior it's on the hood, supercharger, rear wing, front and rear bumper and added everywhere here and there. And yes this is Chev's car with most carbon fibre.

The interior of corvettes are almost identical with the exeption that the ZR1 has alot of carbon fibre here and there.
